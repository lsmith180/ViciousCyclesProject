About Vicious Cycles

Our mission is to provide the best quality services and products to local customers for the life of their bikes. We hope to work with people to educate them and make them comfortable.
We are a locally owned and operated shop located in the Raleigh-Durham area. We specialize in selling quality bicycles, equipment, and gear and offer a variety of repair services and customization options.
At Vicious Cycles, we frequently hold sponsored events and classes, take a look at our schedule and come visit!
